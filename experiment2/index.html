Experiment 2 - Generative Saturn

Description

I created this generative art piece to imitate this image:

Technical

This program uses P5.js. The stars are points scattered randomly across the sky, drawn with a random variety of shades of gray and white. They also fade in and out over time, at randomly varying speeds. Saturn's rings are also randomly placed, with slight variation in color, and move back and forth over time. The terrain in the foreground is generated using Perlin noise.

Reflection

I feel like I could have turned it in less late if I made a more minimal version of it, but I wanted to complete my vision of the project. Getting the rings of Saturn was harder than I thought and took a few iterations, the same with getting the stars to flicker. Also, my project is not very performant, and I suspect it has to do with the rings. If I had more time I would adjust the color of the rings and maybe make the color change over time.
